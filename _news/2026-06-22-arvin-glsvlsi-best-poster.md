Excellent achievement for Arvin and the team. 
Our paper "[Multi-Range Communication for Chiplet-Based Systems](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3787109.3815249)" received the Best Poster Award at 2026 36th Great Lakes Symposium on VLSI (GLSVLSI)!

The paper presents a novel and scalable communication architecture for next-generation large-scale chiplet-based computing systems. This work was conducted as part of the THInK Team at UC Irvine, under the supervision of Prof. Boris Vaisband, in collaboration with Amirtha Chandrasekaran.

Thank you Great Lakes Symposium on VLSI (GLSVLSI) for the recognition!
